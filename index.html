About Kal-Polymers

Kal-Polymers is a prominent recycler and compounder of post-industrial plastics, headquartered in Mississauga, Ontario. With a commitment to sustainability, the company specializes in converting industrial plastic waste into high-quality recycled polymers used across various industries, including automotive, packaging, and consumer goods.

  • Promoting Environmental Sustainability

    By recycling and repurposing industrial plastic waste, Kal-Polymers prevents millions of kilograms of plastics from ending up in landfills, reducing environmental pollution and conserving natural resources.

  • Driving Economic Growth

    The company plays a vital role in the circular economy by creating valuable materials from waste, supporting manufacturers with cost-effective, sustainable raw materials, and generating employment in the recycling sector.

  • Supporting Global Industries

    Kal-Polymers provides innovative solutions to industries such as automotive and packaging, helping them meet their sustainability goals while maintaining product quality and performance.

IT Assistant - Roles and Responsibilities

A detailed overview of my primary responsibilities during my work term at Kal-Polymers.

  • Process Automation
  • Web Application Development
  • Meetings and Demonstrations
  • Requirements Analysis
  • Quality Assurance and Debugging

Process Automation

Streamlining operations and reporting with automation tools.

Automated reports for Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and financial information using Power BI. This automation improved data accuracy and significantly reduced the time required for manual reporting, enabling better decision-making and saving valuable time for stakeholders.

Web Application Development

Developed a real-time data visualization tool for business insights.

Designed and implemented a web application using Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various libraries to display real-time data from the company database. This tool enabled dynamic monitoring of critical metrics on display screens, providing actionable insights at a glance.

Meetings and Demonstrations

Collaborating and presenting progress to stakeholders.

Participated in daily, weekly, and biweekly meetings to discuss progress and address challenges. Conducted sprint reviews with the IT manager every two weeks and demonstrated web application updates to the CEO and senior stakeholders every three weeks.

Requirements Analysis

Understanding and refining product requirements with users.

Collaborated with team members and end-users to gather requirements for web application design and report automation. Engaged in iterative discussions to refine functionalities based on user feedback, ensuring that the delivered solutions met business needs.

Quality Assurance and Debugging

Ensuring product reliability and resolving technical issues.

Conducted rigorous testing to identify and resolve bugs in the web application and automated processes. Ensured high performance, usability, and reliability of deliverables through consistent quality assurance practices.
